Generalized linear models
=========================

This example illustrates the opimization of three linear models:
    * Linear regression (`mlpp.optim.model.ModelLinReg`)
    * Logistic regression (`mlpp.optim.model.ModelLogReg`)
    * Poisson regression (`mlpp.optim.model.ModelPoisReg`)
with five different solvers:
    * LBFGS (`mlpp.optim.solver.BFGS`)
    * SVRG (`mlpp.optim.solver.SVRG`)
    * SDCA (`mlpp.optim.solver.SDCA`)
    * ISTA (`mlpp.optim.solver.Ista`)
    * FISTA (`mlpp.optim.solver.Fista`)
